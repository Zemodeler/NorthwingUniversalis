# Northwing Universalis

Northwing Universalis is a Hearts of Iron IV mod that reimagines the Roblox Napoleonic Era of 2020 as an alternate-history military campaign.

## Overview

The project brings the peak of the Blood and Iron community to Hoi4, with rising nations, old rivalries, and the awakening of powerful factions.

## Story

After the collapse of the kingdom, the Cobalt State is stabilizing under Mateplayss. News arrives that The Crimson Hand is reviving, and the military council answers the threat with the Grand Coalition—a unified front built to confront TCH and secure the State's future.

Not every nation is ready to fight together. Illyria and Aerothia still carry old debts to the Cobalt State, and some leaders see the conflict as a chance for revenge.

At the same time, the newly formed Northwing Kingdom is emerging as a prominent puppet state. Its leaders are beginning to question their loyalty and consider independence, potentially by forming alliances with other aligned states.

## Features

- Community-inspired alternate history scenario
- Coalition warfare against the rising threat of The Crimson Hand
- Political tension between unity, revenge, and independence
- Puppet states and factional intrigue
- Rich narrative backdrop for both multiplayer and single-player play
